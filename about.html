Meet Hannah

Founder

I started Lumora because I believe everyone deserves to feel confident in their skin. Not in a fake, filtered way - but genuinely good about who you are.

Skincare shouldn't be complicated or confusing. It should be accessible, honest, and actually work. That's what we're building here.

When your skin feels good, everything else feels a little easier. That's the goal.

"I want your skin to look as amazing as you deserve to feel—and for you to feel as amazing as your skin looks."

— Hannah

What We Believe

Real Over Perfect

We're not here to sell you impossible standards. We're here to help you understand your skin and work with it, not against it.

Education First

You shouldn't need a chemistry degree to understand your skincare routine. We break it down in a way that actually makes sense.

Your Skin, Your Rules

There's no one-size-fits-all routine. What works for your friend might not work for you, and that's completely fine.
